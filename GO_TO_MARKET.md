# Mindset AI Go-to-Market Strategy

## Phase 1: High-End Hospitality (The Valle de Bravo Pilot)
- **Target:** Luxury boutique hotels and eco-resorts.
- **Goal:** Replace standard concierge with an authorized "Digital Host" that knows every detail of the property and local DNA.
- **Outcome:** Proven efficiency in reducing operational friction and increasing guest spending.

## Phase 2: Mentor Scaling (The "Digital Twin" Launch)
- **Target:** Top-tier business consultants and mindset mentors with established audiences.
- **Goal:** Launch 15-minute "Expert Diagnostic Bots" as high-converting lead magnets.
- **Outcome:** Rapid user acquisition and proof of the "Authorized DNA" scalability.

## Phase 3: Global Enterprise Expansion
- **Target:** International banking and insurance firms.
- **Goal:** Deploy authorized personas for complex client interactions where standard chatbots fail.
- **Outcome:** Dominating the "Digital Mind" infrastructure market globally.

## Key Growth Metrics (North Star)
- Number of authorized "Digital Minds" deployed.
- Interaction fidelity (User satisfaction vs. Human interaction).
- Automated revenue generated for partners.
