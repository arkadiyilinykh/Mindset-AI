# Data Privacy, Ethics & Legal Framework

## 1. Authorized Intelligence Only
- Mindset AI exclusively processes data with explicit legal authorization from the IP holder (Expert or Enterprise).
- We do not scrape public data to create unauthorized clones. 

## 2. Zero-Trace Architecture
- Enterprise data is processed in isolated environments (Single-tenant logic).
- Proprietary business DNA never leaks into public LLM training sets.

## 3. Personality Rights Management
- Blockchain-verified authorization logs for every "Digital Mind" deployed.
- Smart-contract ready frameworks for automated royalty distribution to experts.

## 4. Safety & Guardrails
- Real-time monitoring to prevent the Digital Mind from operating outside its authorized professional domain.
- Multi-layer verification to ensure advice remains high-fidelity and non-hallucinatory.
